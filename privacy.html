Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Paragon Framework ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our website (paragonframework.com and its subdomains, including the product demo) and our digital products (the "Products"). We keep the data we handle to a minimum.

1. Who we are

Paragon Framework is a small independent software studio that sells Flutter application templates and related source code as digital downloads. We are based in Malaysia.

2. Information we collect

  • When you browse our website or demo: our site is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not run our own accounts, forms, or advertising trackers on it. Standard server logs (such as IP address and browser type) may be processed by our hosting provider for security and reliability.
  • When you purchase a Product: payments and delivery are handled by our payment platform, Creem, which acts as the merchant of record. Creem collects the information needed to process your order — such as your name, email address, and payment details — under Creem's Privacy Policy. We receive limited order information (such as your name, email, and what you bought) so we can deliver the Product and provide support.
  • When you contact us: if you email us for support, we receive your email address and whatever you choose to include.

We do not knowingly collect payment card numbers ourselves — those are handled by Creem and its payment processors.

3. How we use information

  • To deliver the Products you purchase and provide license access.
  • To respond to support requests and questions.
  • To send you information about updates to a Product you bought (you can opt out).
  • To comply with legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the service providers that make our business work — principally Creem (payments and delivery) and our email provider — and only as needed for those purposes, or where required by law.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our demo and website do not set advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Creem's checkout, when you make a purchase, uses its own cookies as described in its policy.

6. Data retention

We keep order and support records for as long as needed to provide the Product, honor licenses, and meet legal and tax requirements, after which we delete or anonymize them.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under Malaysia's PDPA and the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to object to certain processing. To make a request, contact us at the address below. You can also exercise rights over purchase data directly with Creem.

8. Children

Our Products and website are intended for developers and businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. International transfers

Our providers may process information in countries other than yours. Where they do, they are expected to protect it in line with applicable law.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: azizi@newheart.com.my.
