FAQ

Does it need Firebase or API keys?

No. Everything runs on built-in demo services. Integration points for Firebase/FCM, payment gateways, and map providers are documented for when you need them.

What license do I get?

A one-time purchase lets you build and ship unlimited apps for yourself, your company, or your clients, and rebrand freely. You may not resell the template's source code itself. Full terms ship in the download and are linked below.

Can I use just one vertical, e.g. only the ride-hailing flow?

Yes — every feature is a self-contained folder. Delete what you don't need.

Is the backend production-ready?

It's a reference implementation (JSON-file storage) designed to be swapped to Postgres/MongoDB — the routes and contracts are the ones the app already uses.

Do I get updates?

Yes. Buy once and get every future version — new features, fixes, and dependency updates — at no extra cost.
